Privacy Policy

Storytelling App is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. This privacy policy outlines how we use any data that you provide while using our app.

Currently, the app is in development mode, and no personal data is collected or shared. The app may request basic information like email and username for testing purposes only.

We use the data for internal testing and app improvements. No information is shared with third parties.

Security

We are committed to ensuring that your data is secure. However, since this is a development version of the app, data security might not be as robust as in the production phase.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Make sure to check this page for any updates.
